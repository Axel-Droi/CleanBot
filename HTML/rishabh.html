About Rishabh Mathukiya

Rishabh Mathukiya is a robotics innovator and technical leader dedicated to building sustainable tech for global change. As co-founder of CleanBot, he manages AI implementation, robotic systems, and field testing. Rishabh is an international robotics competitor and mentor, known for leading robotics programs that educate and empower underserved communities.

He has guided FLL and VEX teams, led international STEM education initiatives, and is recognized for his role in civic outreach, including a mayoral commendation. Rishabh combines environmental consciousness with engineering excellence to make CleanBot a globally scalable solution.
