About Shrivats Pandey

Shrivats Pandey is a robotics and AI enthusiast passionate about sustainable innovation. As a co-founder of CleanBot, he drives both technical development and outreach. Shrivats has won multiple Science Olympiad state championships, developed award-winning robotics systems in FRC (Team 2079), and has a background in engineering, coding, electrical, and media. He also mentors robotics teams, teaches coding to underserved students, and brings leadership to environmental tech initiatives.

Outside of robotics, Shrivats is a certified national pianist, a varsity tennis athlete, and a mentor in international STEM outreach programs. His commitment to innovation, education, and real-world impact drives the mission behind CleanBot.
